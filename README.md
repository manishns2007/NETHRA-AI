# NETHRA-AI

**NETHRA-AI** is an AI-assisted digital investigation platform designed to help investigators analyze digital evidence, automate forensic workflows, and generate actionable intelligence.

Developed for the **Kerala Police Cyberdome**, the platform streamlines the investigation process by combining digital forensics, artificial intelligence, and intelligent evidence management into a unified system.

## Key Highlights

* 🔍 AI-powered digital evidence analysis
* 📂 Secure evidence management with forensic integrity verification
* 🖼️ OCR and image evidence processing
* 🎥 Video evidence analysis and frame extraction
* 🧠 Automated intelligence generation and investigative insights
* 🔗 Correlation of evidence across multiple cases
* 📊 Interactive dashboards and investigation reports
* ⚖️ Chain of custody tracking and audit logging
* 🛡️ Built with a security-first architecture for law enforcement use

> **Built for Kerala Police Cyberdome** to accelerate cybercrime investigations while maintaining forensic integrity, transparency, and scalability.
